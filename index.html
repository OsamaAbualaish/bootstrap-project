maldives

Enjoy the inviting blue waters, beautiful reefs and white sands of the Maldives while staying at the W Maldives. Offering a tranquil escape, the hotel features a diving center, fitness center and outdoor infinity pool. .

A City to Fall in Love With . venice

Visit the Rialto Market and the charming Rialto Bridge and then take a gondola ride through the canals before learning how Venetian and Murano glass is made. The city’s churches, piazzas and museums are all worth a visit and can be easily reached within a short walk.

Toronto

The most populous city in Canada has many cultural experiences waiting for visitors, including the Art Gallery of Ontario, the Ontario Science Centre, or the secret passageways of Casa Loma. The Hockey Hall of Fame is a testament to this sport's widespread popularity in Ontario. Fans are encouraged to see the Toronto Maple Leafs play at the Air Canada Centre.

Hiking at Tour Du Mont Blanc

in France, Italy, Switzerland This famous long-distance trek in Europe affords ardent adventurers the rare opportunity to traverse three of the continent's most charming countries in a once-in-a-lifetime expedition. Teeming with picturesque scenery, it passes through gorgeous alpine meadows, rare mountain passes.

paris

When we think of Paris, we often imagine couples in love, à la Edith Piaf singing “Sous le ciel de Paris” or “La vie en rose“, or kissing, as in Robert Doisneau’s famous photo “The Kiss by the Hôtel de Ville”.
